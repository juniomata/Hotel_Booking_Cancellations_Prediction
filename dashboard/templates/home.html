Bell-Man

Booking Cancellation Prediction Machine

About


Bell-Man (Booking Cancellation Prediction Machine) is a platform to predict hotel booking cancellations. This platform is built from the analysis and modeling of the hotel demand dataset in Portugal. This project is initiated by the fact that booking cancellations have a significant impact on demand-management decisions in the hospitality industry. They limit the production of accurate forecasts, a critical tool in terms of revenue management performance.

Bell-Man will help the hoteliers to identify which bookings are likely to be cancelled. By knowing the guest who is likely to cancel the bookings, the hoteliers can do the right actions and adjust their overbooking tactics and cancellation policies appropriately to increase the revenue.
